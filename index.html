Work

DESIGN THINKING

[Innovation for Disadvantaged People]

This is a design thinking created by me and my group members, it is about an healthcare app named "Evercare". It is most useful for elderlies with only a phone, and its features can be utilized completely if paired with a smartwatch.

The video link below showcases and explains the app's features: Evercare features

My role in the project is to interview elderlies to understand their problems regarding their health and brainstorm solutions to create an app that aligns with their needs. In addition, I take part in the role of video editing as I have the experience of editing short video clips for entertainment in my daily life.

DIGITAL LOGIC PROJECT

[Network Packet Transmission Monitoring System]

In this project, user is required to choose a single digit number of packet data that user wanted to transfer from computers of lab A to computers of lab B. User can control manually the packet data transfer from one of the six computers from lab A to one of the six computers from lab B. Transfer of packet data will stop when the number of packet data transferred reached the number user chosen.

I really appreciate this opportunity to create a circuit with a real-life setting in Deeds with teammates. Although the circuit our team created is just a simple circuit compared to the circuit or simulation I researched online, there are still challenges to tackle and lessons to learn while working on this project. This indicates that there is still so much more for me to learn and I will no doubt feed into the knowledge hunger.
